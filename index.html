Efo riro

Introduction

Efo Riro is a rich vegetable soup and a very popular in the Yoruba area in Nigeria. This is one of my best recipes and it is one of my favorite Nigerian soups because of its health benefits... It’s stressless with low time and great reuslt: just a handful of Iru (locust beans), crayfish, ginger & garlic and other spices gives it the most incredible flavour.

Efo riro is a one-pot stew with layers of flavor. When Efo riro is cooking, the aroma is always fascinating very attractive. Very Attractive!!!

How to Serve Efo riro

There are three major ways to serve Efo riro.

  1. Efo riro can be serverd alone as a meal(dried) or with other sauce.
  2. Efo riro can also be served as watery (efo riro peppersoup)
  3. and the other way is serving along side with light or heavy swallow. Swallow of different types as desired such as Semovita, Eba, Pounded Yam or Amala or Fufu as well. On the lighter side, you can serve this over Rice, Pap (Eko) or boiled Yam.

Side Dishes

A few things that would be very much at home alongside Efo riro:

  • Emu (palm wine) / chilled drink
  • Asun / Assorted meat / Bush meat
  • Roasted or Grilled Chicken / Turkey
